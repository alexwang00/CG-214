Midterm Proposal

For my midterm, I want to make a website showcasing concert photos I've taken over the past few years.
The photos will include portraits and in-concert action shots of different hip-hop artists.
The website will consist of three pages:

  • a home page with all the photos
  • a page with embedded songs and music videos from the respective artists
  • a page with brief bios and one photo of each hip-hop artist featured on the home page

As a photography major, I'd like to present my concert photos I've shot throughout the last
couple years. I already have a portfolio website featuring all of my work but I want to create a
website that focuses only on my music photography. Music and concert photography are appealing
to me because of the energy I get to capture during the shows. Also, rappers are one of my favorite
subjects to shoot because they are usually styled more lavishly than regular subjects and bring a special
kind of energy/vibe into the photo.
