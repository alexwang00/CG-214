DOM KENNEDY

Dom Kennedy is a 32 year-old hip-hop artist from Leimert Park, a section of
Los Angeles, California. He has released eight mixtapes and three albums
dating back to 2008. In the hip-hop world and especially in California, Dom is
known for his smooth flow and laid-back style that he consistently brings to
every track he raps on. While some songs have gained mainstream recognition,
Dom has remained relatively off the radar to those who don't follow hip-hop.
He has always remained an independent artist and even started his own
label called OPM (Other People's Money) to which he has signed several
artists. His most popular mixtapes among fans are 25th Hours, From the
Westside with Love, and Yellow Album. In the past, he has collaborated with
artists like Kendrick Lamar, Casey Veggies, Schoolboy Q, and Rick Ross.
