A$AP FERG

A$AP Ferg is a 28 year-old hip-hop artist from Harlem, New York. He's most
known for being a member of A$AP mob with A$AP Rocky and a few other
members. He is signed to Polo Grounds, RCA, and his main label A$AP
Worldwide started by members of A$AP. Ferg Forever is the only mixtape
he's ever released and Trap Lord and Always Strive and Prosper are his only
two studio albums. In the past, Ferg has collaborated with Schoolboy Q, Big
Sean, Future, and many other artists. He hit mainstream success with songs
like "Work" and "Shabba" on his album Trap Lord. Currently, Ferg is on tour in
America with Playboi Carti, the only rapper A$AP has ever signed to their
Harlem-based roster.
