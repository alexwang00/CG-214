TRAVIS SCOTT

Travis Scott is a 24 year-old hip-hop artist from Houston, Texas.
He's known for his energetic music and wild performances. Scott
got his start in the industry after T.I. discovered him in Los Angeles
and signed him to his label, Grand Hustle. In addition to Grand Hustle,
Scott is signed to Epic Records, and GOOD Beats. He has four projects
to his name: Owl Pharaoh, Days Before Rodeo, Rodeo, and Birds in the
Trap Sing McKnight. His most notable collaborators include Kid Cudi,
Kanye West, Young Thug, Migos, Metro Boomin, Future, and 2 Chainz.
